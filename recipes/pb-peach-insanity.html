Peanut Butter Peach Insanity Smoothie

Possibly my favorite smoothie ever.

Serves: 1

Ingredients

  • 1 medium banana, ideally frozen (118g)
  • 2 tablespoons powdered peanut butter (13g)
  • 1 scoop vanilla whey protein powder (31g)
  • ⅔ cup unsweetened vanilla almond milk (150g)
  • ⅔ cup Greek yogurt (160g)
  • ¾ cup frozen peach (100g)

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Serve chilled. Optionally top with a few slices of peach or a sprinkle of cinnamon.

Macros (per serving)

Total

  • Calories: 424 kcal
  • Protein: 52g
  • Carbs: 46g
  • Fat: 5g
