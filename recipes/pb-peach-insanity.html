Peanut Butter Peach Insanity Smoothie

Possibly my favorite smoothie ever.

Serves: 1

Ingredients

  • 1 medium banana (118g)
  • 13g powdered peanut butter
  • 31g vanilla whey protein powder
  • 150g unsweetened vanilla almond milk
  • 160g Greek yogurt
  • 100g frozen peach

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Serve chilled. Optionally top with a few slices of peach or a sprinkle of cinnamon.

Macros (per serving)

Total

  • Calories: 424 kcal
  • Protein: 52g
  • Carbs: 46g
  • Fat: 5g
