Garlic Butter Chicken Thighs with Turmeric Rice

A flavorful one-pan meal that’s great for meal prep or dinner. Optional garlic yogurt sauce makes it even better.

Serves: 4

Prep time: 10 minutes Cook time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

For the Chicken & Rice

  • 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs (4–5 thighs, 680g)
  • 1 ⅓ cups white or jasmine rice (uncooked) (250g)
  • 3 cups chicken broth or water (720g)
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped (optional) (110g)
  • 4–5 garlic cloves, minced (15g)
  • 1 ½ tablespoons butter (21g)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (13g)
  • 1 ½ tablespoons soy sauce (22g)
  • 1 tablespoon paprika (5g)
  • 1 tablespoon turmeric (5g)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin (2g)
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • ½ cup frozen peas (optional, also not included in my macros because I couldn't be bothered) (75g)
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

For the Garlic Yogurt Sauce (optional)

  • ½ cup plain Greek yogurt (120g)
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice (10g)
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil (4g)
  • 1 small garlic clove, finely grated or minced (3g)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional: pinch of oregano or Italian seasoning
  • Optional: 1–2 teaspoons sriracha for spice (5–10g)

Instructions

  1. Season the Chicken: Season chicken thighs with salt, pepper, paprika, and cumin.
  2. Sear the Chicken: Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Sear chicken thighs for 3–4 minutes per side until golden. Remove and set aside.
  3. Sauté Aromatics: In the same pan, add butter and onion (if using). Sauté for 3–5 minutes until softened. Add garlic and turmeric, stir for 30 seconds.
  4. Toast the Rice: Add rice and stir to coat in butter and spices. Toast for 1 minute.
  5. Add Liquid & Simmer: Pour in chicken broth and soy sauce. Stir well and bring to a simmer. Nestle chicken thighs on top.
  6. Cover & Cook: Cover the pan, reduce heat to low, and cook for 18–20 minutes (or longer if using larger chicken pieces). Add peas in the last 5 minutes, if using.
  7. Rest & Fluff: Turn off heat and let sit, covered, for 5 minutes. Fluff rice with a fork.
  8. Make the Sauce (optional): Mix all yogurt sauce ingredients in a bowl. Adjust seasoning. Stir in sriracha if desired.
  9. Serve or Store: Serve immediately or portion into meal prep containers. Drizzle yogurt sauce on top or serve on the side. Garnish with parsley.
