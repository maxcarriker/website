Anabolic Pancakes

High-protein pancakes that actually taste good. Great for breakfast or meal prep.

Serves: 1

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup low-fat cottage cheese (169.5g)
  • 2 large eggs (100g)
  • ⅓ cup egg whites (92g)
  • ½ cup flour (62.5g)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (4.2g)
  • ½ tsp baking powder (2.3g)

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Pour onto a heated nonstick pan or griddle and cook like regular pancakes — a few minutes per side until golden brown.
  3. Serve warm. Top with syrup or fruit if desired.

Macros (per serving)

Total

  • Calories: 554 kcal
  • Protein: 50g
  • Carbs: 55g
  • Fat: 12g
